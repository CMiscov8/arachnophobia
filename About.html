What the SpIDer app does

The SpIDer app allows you to identify spiders using the camera feature.

  • If you have a low tolerance for spiders, the camera will not display, it will simply tell you when the image is matchable.

  • If you have a medium tolerance for spiders, the camera will show a cartoon spider within the viewfinder

  • If you have a high tolerance for spiders - perhaps you are an arachnophile using the app for indentification – the camera will act normally, showing the spider within the viewfinder

The Psychology behind the SpIDer App

I used to be terrified of spiders. I couldn't look at pictures of spiders, and definitely couldn't touch spiders, even if they were squished. However, I found the by identifying and naming the spiders I came across, I reduced the fear I felt considerably. Taming by Naming. As well, by categorizing spiders as safe, mildly harmful, or deadly, we allow for a more rational approach to their appearance in our lives.

I still don't like spiders, but I no longer feel the need to throw my phone across the room whenever a picture of one comes through my feed. I even touched a little plastic one with by bare hands!!! Now that's what I call progress.

Disclaimer

Although I have tried my best to make the app as accurate as possible with the technology available, identification by visual matching is as yet an imperfect science. Please use caution around unknown species of spiders, and double check identification with a secondary source before approaching them.
